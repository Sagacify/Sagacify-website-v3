QueryZest

A smart Q&A platform bringing knowledge seekers and knowledge experts together

  • Context

    Innovation basically starts from the interaction and exchange between smart minds. To survive in this fast-paced and competitive global market, companies have to make the most of the pool of employee knowledge and expertise at their disposal.

  • Problem

    Employees face issues, problems or challenges at work everyday. There is a high probability, however, of the solution to their problem lying in the hands of a colleague working at the same organisation.

    To nurture innovation and collaboration, organisations have to find ways to bring these people together before they waste time trying to find the answer from an external source.

  • Solution

    QueryZest is an intelligent question and answer system. Intelligent in the sense that when a user sends a question, that question is automatically redirected and pushed into the mailbox of people most likely to know the answer.

    Using state of the art machine learning and semantic analysis, QueryZest gains deeper insights and learns from user expertise profiles the more questions and answers are submitted to it.

    QueryZest works by default using emails, but can be connected (using Webhooks) to any systems such as entreprise social networks.
