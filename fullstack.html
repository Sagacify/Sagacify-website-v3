Full stack developer

Sagacify is a web development company, based on our passion and product expertise at the intersection of mainly two domains: data science/machine learning and software development. By applying complex algorithms we reveal new data patterns and relationships in Big Data, structuring complex data to maximize its value within an organization.

We are looking for a highly intelligent, curious, creative and collaborative full stack developer to join Sagacify’s growing team of immensely talented, all-around awesome people. You need to be comfortable working with large scale architecture to help us build our products and put them into production in the cloud. Applicants should be desirably enthusiastic about web technology, scalability, data efficiency, and code quality.

Required Skills

  • Strong JavaScript knowledge (Node.js, Backbone.js, React.js, jQuery).
  • Experience with NoSQL databases (MongoDB, Elasticsearch) and SQL (PostgreSQL).
  • Ability to architect complex web applications and API structures.

Profile

  • You thrive on solving complex problems with a combination of code, brute force, and ingenuity.
  • Your average week includes working on web, databases, architecting, debugging.
  • You love writing code.
  • You stay current on the latest tech trends.
  • You’re a team player who likes to engage with colleagues at a professional and personal level.
  • You can handle a high volume of requests with frequent interruptions and prioritize your own work while taking infrastructure and business needs into account.

What We can offer You

  • Competitive salary
  • Flexible hours
  • Free coffee, and fruits
  • Video gaming during lunch breaks
  • No long winding meetings
  • Regular team events/excursions
