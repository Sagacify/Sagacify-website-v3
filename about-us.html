  • Our story

    We are in the midst of the information age. Data is everywhere and is exponentially growing, encapsulating most of the world’s knowledge. Technology, on the other hand, is progressing at an incredible pace. In this fast changing environment, our mission is to create technological and data-driven solutions to help your organisation make more informed decisions.

  • What we do

    Data science / Big data

    Creating data driven solutions: We, at Sagacify, help companies make sense of their data by revealing valuable insights that help them make more informed decisions.

    Mobile and web software development

    We create state of the art web and mobile solutions to help you stay in control in this fast changing digital environment.

  • Our people

    We are a tight-knit team with a passion for data. Our team consists of data scientists, software developers, entrepreneurs and designers that blur the lines between these disciplines to get the most out of data. We love building simple-to-use applications and software that solve complex problems facing today’s tech world.

  • Our culture

    Holacracy! At Sagacify, it’s all about the people. We have chosen not to follow the traditional hierarchical organisational model. Everyone is free to speak up, suggest creative approaches and contribute to solving a problem.

  • Universitiy collaboration

    Sagacify collaborates with Université catholique de Louvain (UCL) and Université libre de Bruxelles (ULB) to stay at the top of academic research within the field of Data Science and help foster the academic field. We help bridging the gap between academic research and their application in real world solutions.
